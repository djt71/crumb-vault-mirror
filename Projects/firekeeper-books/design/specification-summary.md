# Wisdom Library — Phase 1 Specification Summary

**Mission:** Premium illustrated digital editions of public domain texts — fiction, drama, poetry, philosophy. Illustrations are the moat. Revenue validates reach.

**Phase 1 scope:** Ebook only. Fiction-first strategy: Frankenstein (Title #1), then The Odyssey (Title #2, timed to Nolan film July 17, 2026). Design system must work across genres from the start. Audiobook (Phase 2) and multilingual (Phase 3) deferred.

## Key Decisions

- **Series name:** Firekeeper Books. Domain `firekeeperbooks.com` registered. USPTO clear.
- **Fiction-first:** Illustrations are the moat, fiction maximizes it. Meditations deferred (Stoicism shelf saturated).
- **Distribution:** Wide from day one. Direct sales (firekeeperbooks.com) + KDP + Kobo + Google Play + Apple Books (direct, not D2D — D2D prohibits PD).
- **Pricing:** $7.99 (revised from $5.99 — 35% KDP PD royalty makes higher price necessary).
- **KDP royalty:** 35% for PD content (~$2.80/sale). Non-Amazon channels yield $5.59+. Direct sales ~$7.59. Wide distribution is the economic foundation.
- **AI tools:** Comparative evaluation at M-1/M0: Midjourney, Nano Banana 2, DALL-E. Not locked to one tool.
- **M0 is a hard gate** with human-illustrator fallback if AI tools can't deliver.

## Title #1: Frankenstein — Mary Shelley

1818 text (first edition). 16–20 original AI illustrations. Gothic aesthetic. No truly illustrated Kindle Frankenstein at our quality bar — original illustrations are the market gap.

## Title #2: The Odyssey — Homer

Translation TBD (Butler, Pope, Cowper, or Lawrence). 28–32 illustrations. Timed to Nolan film (July 17, 2026). Target publish: late June 2026.

## Revenue Model

Catalog-compounding play. At $7.99/35% KDP, per-sale Amazon revenue is ~$2.80. Non-Amazon channels and direct sales are where margins live (blended ~$4.02/sale). Real economics emerge at 10–20 titles. Multi-year arc.

## Validation

30–40 unit sales at $7.99 in 60–90 days = minimum viable. <10 sales + no reviews = kill. Marketing treated as learning variable. Go/no-go after 2 titles + data.

## Critical Path

(1) AI illustration consistency — hard gate at M0. (2) Odyssey timing — ship before/around July 17. (3) Schedule compression — Frankenstein pipeline must be efficient enough to leave room for Odyssey production. Track actual hours via `progress/time-log.md`.

## Risk

Very low conflict safety risk (9/10). AI art copyright ambiguous but not blocking. LLC deferred to post-M3. PIIA reviewed — Exhibit B carve-out protects this activity.
