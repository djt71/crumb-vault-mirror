# Think Different — Master Roster

Quick-reference table of all personalities featured in Apple's Think Different campaign (1997–2002). Links point to individual profile files in the `profiles/` directory.

## Campaign Media Key

- **TV** — 60-second television commercial ("Here's to the Crazy Ones")
- **Print** — Print advertisements and posters (5 series + standalone)
- **Edu** — Educator series (distributed to schools)
- **Promo** — Promotional variant (cross-promotion)
